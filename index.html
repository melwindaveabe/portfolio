This system is online booking of ten specific services. The system has three types of users: admin, provider, and customer. The provider will register their shop and select a specific service from the 10 available services. Each service has its own specific offerings, such as cleaning. After registering, they will submit the required supporting documents, which will then be verified by the admin. Once verified, the shops will be visible to customers, and they can be booked if the chosen shop still has available slots..
